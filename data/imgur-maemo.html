imgur on Maemo

Welcome to the imgur.com integration on Maemo. Eventually there will be a proper application here, but for now there is this help file.

How to enable imgur integration

  1. Run the "Settings" application.
  2. Tap "Sharing accounts".
  3. Press the "New" button.
  4. Choose imgur from the list.
  5. Press the "Validate" button.

You do not need to give any configuration information, such as a username or password.

There is currently no way to turn it off again without uninstalling the package.

How to upload images

  1. Look at the image in the image viewer.
  2. Press the sharing button. It looks like this: (picture here)
  3. Press "Share via service".
  4. If the "Account" button does not say "imgur.com" on it, press it, and choose imgur from the list.
  5. Press "Share".
  6. Your image should be loaded into the web browser. It will look something like this one.
